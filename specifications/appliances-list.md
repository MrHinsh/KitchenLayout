## Refrigerator

**Make:** Samsung  
**Model:** **RF32CGSN10B1**  
**Type:** Full-height French-door refrigerator with bottom freezer drawer  
**Location:** Tall Cabinetry Wall, **908 mm dedicated section**  
**Installation type:** Freestanding, visually integrated

### Dimensions (external)

* **Width:** 908 mm
* **Height:** 1,778 mm
* **Depth:** 861 mm
* **Effective depth incl. door clearance:** ~910 mm

### Installation requirements

* **Electrical:** 110 V, standard household outlet (adjacent cabinet)
* **Water:** Not required (no ice or water dispenser)
* **Side clearance:** 10–20 mm each side
* **Top clearance:** ≥25 mm
* **Rear clearance:** ≥25 mm (airflow and power cable)
* **Floor:** Level, capable of supporting fully loaded appliance
* **Ventilation:** Passive ventilation required if boxed in

### Cabinet maker instructions

* Provide a **clear internal width of 908 mm minimum**, excluding fillers.
* Do **not** clamp the refrigerator tightly between panels.
* If flanked by tall units, include:
  * Either a **vented plinth** below, or
  * A **vent slot or shadow gap** above.
* Allow full door swing of both French doors and freezer drawer.
* Side panels to stop **short of door hinge plane** to avoid interference.
* Appliance remains **freestanding**, no fixing to cabinetry.

## Hob (Cooktop)

**Brand:** IO Mabe  
**Model:** **PIO90SMXN0**  
**Series:** Tecnología Ultimate  
**Type:** Built-in gas hob, black tempered glass  
**Location:** Continuous base cabinetry  
**Installation type:** Drop-in

### Dimensions (overall)

* **Width:** 900 mm
* **Depth:** 510 mm
* **Height:** 130 mm

### Specifications

* **Burners:** 5
* **Fuel:** Gas
* **Surface:** Black tempered glass
* **Controls:** Front-mounted knobs

### Cut-out dimensions (estimated – must be verified)

* **Width:** 860–870 mm
* **Depth:** 490–500 mm
* **Corner radius:** R5–R10 mm

⚠️ **Critical:** IO Mabe does not publish official cut-out dimensions.  
**Measure the metal chassis under the glass before cutting granite.**

### Installation requirements

* Gas supply to local code
* Front and underside ventilation as required
* Granite cutting required
* Black powder-coated aluminium infill at front edge

### Cabinet maker instructions

* Design base cabinet run assuming **90 cm hob width**, drawers below.
* Coordinate with stone fabricator **before cabinet finalisation**.
* Allow for a **full-width matte black powder-coated aluminium infill strip** at the **front of the hob cut-out** to correct depth difference.
* Ensure cabinet rails do **not obstruct hob body or gas connection**.
* No oven below, drawers only.
* Do not rely on glass overhang for structural support.

## Dishwasher

**Make:** Samsung  
**Model:** **DW80CG5420SREM**  
**Type:** Fully integrated built-in dishwasher  
**Location:** Continuous base cabinetry  
**Finish:** Stainless steel (panel-ready compatible)

### Dimensions

* **Width:** 598 mm
* **Height:** 845 mm (adjustable feet, approx. 835–870 mm range)
* **Depth:** 600 mm (excluding door panel)

### Cabinet opening required

* **Width:** 600 mm
* **Height:** 850 mm nominal
* **Depth:** ≥610 mm (hoses and power)

### Installation requirements

* **Electrical:** 120 V / 60 Hz, grounded outlet in adjacent cabinet
* **Water:** Cold water, ¾″ connection
* **Drain:** High loop or air gap as per local code
* **Ventilation:** Front venting via toe-kick

### Cabinet maker instructions

* Provide **exact 600 mm clear opening**, no fillers intruding.
* Door to accept **custom Pirota wood panel** aligned with adjacent drawers.
* Maintain clearance for **AutoRelease™ door opening**.
* Ensure toe-kick is **removable and aligned** with recessed black plinth.
* No side ventilation required.
