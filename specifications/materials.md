## Materials Specification

### Cabinet construction

* **Carcases:**

  * **Madera** (engineered wood board), suitable for cabinetry.
  * All panels sealed on **all faces and edges**, including backs.
* **Fronts:**

  * **Parota veneer–covered fronts** (Parota appearance).
  * Flat, modern, handleless design.

### Finish and treatment

* **Colour:** Dark natural Parota tone, grain visible.
* **Finish system:**

  * **Matte sheen**
  * Fully sealed and suitable for **humid tropical and coastal conditions** (Cancún).
  * Moisture-resistant and mould-resistant.
* **Edge treatment:**

  * All edges sealed, no exposed substrate.

### Countertops

* **Existing granite worktop retained.**
* Only localised cutting or adjustment permitted where specified (hob infill).
* No replacement or refinishing assumed.

### Hardware

* **Drawer runners:**

  * Heavy-duty, full-extension, soft-close.
  * Corrosion-resistant.
* **Hinges:**

  * Soft-close.
  * Rated for humid and coastal environments.
* **Handles:**

  * None.
  * Handleless achieved through reveals, shadow gaps, or finger pulls.

### Sealants and adhesives

* All glues, sealants, and bonding agents to be:

  * Rated for **humid tropical climates**.
  * Resistant to delamination.
  * **Food-safe** where applicable.

### General build requirements

* Allow for **seasonal movement** between carcass and veneered fronts.
* No unsealed end grain anywhere in the build.
* All fixings to be corrosion-resistant.
* Workmanship suitable for long-term use in a coastal Cancún environment.
