# Tall Cabinetry Wall

![Tall Cabinetry Wall Render](images/renders/section-01-tall-wall/overview.png)

## Tall Cabinetry Wall – General Instructions

### Dimensions
- Height: Floor to ceiling  
- Depth: Match adjacent tall cabinetry  

### Materials and finish
- **Parota wood**, dark natural tone, handleless  
- Consistent reveals and shadow gaps across all tall cabinetry  

### Services
- Service voids **only where required** by appliance specifications or electrical code  
- Electrical and ventilation to follow manufacturer requirements  
- All electrical work to comply with Mexican codes  

### Visual intent
- Tall cabinetry to read as a single continuous wall  
- No visible filler panels on the front face  

---

## Tall Storage Section

**Cabinet width:** 600 mm  
**Height:** Floor to ceiling  
**Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Configuration:**
- Lower section: **deep drawers** from floor level upward (full-extension, deep format for general storage)  
- At a practical ergonomic height, drawers transition into a **full-height cabinet**  
- Upper cabinet: single full-height door or split doors as required by height, internal layout configurable for pantry-style storage (adjustable shelves)  
- Upper cabinet continues uninterrupted to the ceiling  

**Visual intent:** Section must read as a clean vertical volume with clean transition from drawers to cabinet with no visual breaks  

---

## Oven & Microwave Tower

**Cabinet width:** 800 mm  
**Height:** Floor to ceiling  
**Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Appliance configuration:**
- **Oven:** Integrated electric oven at standard ergonomic height (220 V dedicated circuit)  
- **Microwave:** Freestanding microwave, visually integrated, installed directly above oven at head height (110 V, may be shared)  
- Appliances must be vertically aligned and centred within the cabinet  
- Microwave installed using **recessed shadow-box opening** with uniform 5–10 mm reveal on all sides  
- Respect all manufacturer ventilation clearances  
- Microwave must be **fully removable** without dismantling cabinetry  
- **No panels, doors, or cladding** applied to the microwave (trim kits only if explicitly supported by model)  

**Cabinet configuration:**
- **Lower section:** Drawers from floor level up to oven base  
- **Upper section:** Full-height cabinet above microwave extending to ceiling (use vertical lift-up door where height allows safe operation, or split into two vertically aligned doors if too tall)  

**Electrical & services:**
- 220 V circuit dedicated to oven, 110 V supply for microwave may be shared  
- Outlets accessible without dismantling cabinetry  
- Circuits clearly labelled in electrical panel  
- Service voids only where required by appliance or electrical code  
- Heat shielding where required by oven manufacturer  

**Visual intent:** Oven and microwave to read as a single integrated vertical composition, section width to visually match the 800 mm appliance storage section

## Refrigerator Section

**Appliance width:** 908 mm (Samsung RF32CGSN10B1)  
**Overall cabinet width:** 908 mm minimum clear opening + side clearances  
**Height:** Floor to ceiling  
**Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Configuration:**
- **Refrigerator:** Full-height French-door refrigerator with bottom freezer drawer installed as freestanding but visually integrated, centred within opening with manufacturer-specified clearances maintained  
- **Upper cabinet:** Cabinet above refrigerator extending fully to ceiling (split doors if required by height)  

**Ventilation and services:**
- Ventilation per refrigerator manufacturer requirements  
- Electrical outlet 110 V (shared supply acceptable), outlet to remain accessible  
- Circuit to be labelled in the electrical panel  

**Visual intent:** Refrigerator to read as a primary integrated appliance, upper cabinet completes the vertical rhythm of the wall

## Appliance Storage (Appliance Garage)

**Cabinet width:** 800 mm  
**Height:** Floor to ceiling  
**Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Lower section:** Drawers from floor level up to standard counter height (deep-format drawers for small appliance and general storage, drawer heights aligned visually with adjacent base cabinetry)  

**Upper section:** Full-height appliance storage behind doors from counter height to ceiling, intended for coffee station and small appliances with adjustable internal shelving sized for coffee machine, grinder, and accessories, integrated warm LED lighting  

**Doors:** Side-hinged bi-fold doors (two leaves per side) that open outward and fold flat against cabinet side panels, park fully open held with magnetic or mechanical stays. **No pocket doors, no roller systems, no lift-up mechanisms**. Grain direction matched across doors and aligned with adjacent cabinetry.  

**Electrical & services:**
- Internal 110 V outlets for appliances (see [electrical.md](specifications/electrical.md)), outlets concealed but accessible  
- Ventilation appropriate for coffee machines and small appliances  

**Visual intent:** Closed - reads as a single uninterrupted cabinet volume. Open - doors do not obstruct access or project into circulation space. Width balanced with the 800 mm oven/microwave tower.
