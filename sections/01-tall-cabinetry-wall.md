# Tall Cabinetry Wall

![Tall Cabinetry Wall Render](images/renders/section-01-tall-wall/overview.png)

## Tall Cabinetry Wall – General Instructions

### Dimensions
- Height: Floor to ceiling  
- Depth: Match adjacent tall cabinetry  

### Materials and finish
- **Parota wood**, dark natural tone, handleless  
- Consistent reveals and shadow gaps across all tall cabinetry  

### Services
- Service voids **only where required** by appliance specifications or electrical code  
- Electrical and ventilation to follow manufacturer requirements  
- All electrical work to comply with Mexican codes  

### Visual intent
- Tall cabinetry to read as a single continuous wall  
- No visible filler panels on the front face

## Tall Storage Section

**Cabinet width:** 600 mm

- **Height:** Floor to ceiling  
- **Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Configuration:**
- Lower section to consist of **deep drawers** from floor level upward  
- At a practical ergonomic height, drawers transition into a **full-height cabinet**  
- Upper cabinet continues uninterrupted to the ceiling  

### Drawer specification
- Full-extension, deep format for general storage  

### Upper cabinet specification
- Single full-height door or split doors as required by height  
- Internal layout configurable for pantry-style storage (adjustable shelves)  

### Visual intent
- Section must read as a clean vertical volume  
- Clean transition from drawers to cabinet with no visual breaks

## Oven & Microwave Tower

**Cabinet width:** 800 mm

- **Height:** Floor to ceiling  
- **Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Appliance configuration:**
- **Oven:** Integrated electric oven, installed at standard ergonomic oven height  
  - **Power:** 220 V  
  - **Circuit:** Dedicated  
- **Microwave:** Freestanding microwave, visually integrated, installed directly above the oven at head height  
  - **Power:** 110 V  
  - **Supply:** May be shared  

Appliances must be vertically aligned and centred within the cabinet  

### Microwave integration
- Microwave to be installed using a **recessed shadow-box opening**  
- Maintain a **uniform 5–10 mm reveal** on all sides  
- Respect all **manufacturer ventilation clearances**  
- Microwave must be **fully removable** without dismantling cabinetry  
- **No panels, doors, or cladding** applied to the microwave  
- Trim kits only to be used if explicitly supported by the selected model  

### Lower section
- Drawers from floor level up to oven base  

### Upper section
- Full-height cabinet above the microwave, extending to the ceiling  
- Use **vertical lift-up door** where door height allows safe operation  
- If door is too tall, split into two vertically aligned doors  

### Electrical
- **220 V circuit dedicated to the oven**  
- **110 V supply for microwave may be shared**  
- Outlets to be accessible without dismantling cabinetry  
- Circuits to be clearly labelled in the electrical panel  

### Services and safety
- Service voids only where required by appliance or electrical code  
- Heat shielding where required by oven manufacturer  

### Visual intent
- Oven and microwave to read as a **single integrated vertical composition**  
- Section width to visually match the **800 mm appliance storage section**

## Appliance Storage (Appliance Garage)

**Cabinet width:** 800 mm

- **Height:** Floor to ceiling  
- **Depth:** Match adjacent tall cabinetry

**Lower section:**
- Drawers from floor level up to **standard counter height**  
- Deep-format drawers for small appliance and general storage  
- Drawer heights aligned visually with adjacent base cabinetry  

### Upper section
- Full-height appliance storage behind doors, from counter height to ceiling  
- Intended for **coffee station and small appliances**  
- Adjustable internal shelving sized for coffee machine, grinder, and accessories  
- Integrated **warm LED lighting**  

### Doors
- **Side-hinged bi-fold doors**, two leaves per side  
- Doors open outward and **fold flat against the cabinet side panels**  
- Doors **park fully open**, held with magnetic or mechanical stays  
- **No pocket doors, no roller systems, no lift-up mechanisms**  
- Grain direction matched across doors and aligned with adjacent cabinetry  

### Electrical
- Internal **110 V outlets** for appliances (see electrical.md)  
- Outlets concealed but accessible  

### Services and ventilation
- Ventilation appropriate for coffee machines and small appliances  

### Visual intent
- Closed: reads as a **single uninterrupted cabinet volume**  
- Open: doors do not obstruct access or project into circulation space  
- Width balanced with the **800 mm oven/microwave tower**
